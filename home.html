About Us

Clear Currents aspires to be at the forefront of addressing Canada’s water crisis, pioneering a transformative mission that integrates education, media, and sustainability. We utilize interactive media by donating 1$ for every engagement our mini-games receive towards supporting sustainable water solutions. Before we can say we are working towards a sustainable world we must have a sustainable and livable community here in Canada.

Our Vision

Clear Currents’ overarching goal is to secure a future where access to clean water is not only a basic right but a shared reality for all Canadians. Furthermore, we hope to transcend borders and make this an international goal as well. Support us through this journey by donating or engaging with our interactive platform.
